Apple confirms that government agencies are investigating secret iPhone throttling

2018-01-31 newsbot agencies, news, secret

“Other governmental agencies and consumer groups in various international markets, including Brazil, China, France, Italy, and South Korea, are looking for answers from Apple as well” writes Maren Estrada for bgr.com. Apple late on Tuesday confirmed that unspecified governmental agencies are currently investigating the iPhone slowdown scandal, a few hours after a report said that the Department of Justice and the Securities and Exchange Commission have both asked the iPhone maker for more information about the matter.
