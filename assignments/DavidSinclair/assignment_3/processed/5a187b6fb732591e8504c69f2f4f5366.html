In this video, Lilo discusses Gamers Are Smarter Than Average!

Studies show that gamers are smarter on average than non-gamers. They are more creative and less stressed Gamers also do better in school, and have better perceptual abilities than non-gaming peers. The United States government has said gamers perform 10-20% better in cognitive ability. Video games also improve fluid intelligence.
