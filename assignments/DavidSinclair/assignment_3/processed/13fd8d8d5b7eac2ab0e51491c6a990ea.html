Further Education Commissioner: annual report 2016 to 2017

Report of the activity of the Further Education (FE) Commissioner between 1 September 2016 and 31 August 2017.

Further Education Commissioner: annual report 2016 to 2017

Ref: DFE-00009-2018 PDF, 363KB, 17 pages

Details

The Further Education Commissioner reports on his activity between 1 September 2016 and 31 August 2017, and on the health of the further education college sector. He identifies challenges for the coming year and new initiatives designed to meet them.
